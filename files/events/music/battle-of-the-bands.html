Brief

Battle of the Bands is here to bring you the thrill of a music concert and the high-octane excitement of watching a live performance. Bands of up to ten members will rock the stage, either covering songs or performing their original compositions. They will have 15 minutes to dazzle the crowds and woo the judges. Every band should put their best strum, tap, hum forward, because the winning team will receive a prize worth Rs. 50,000 and the chance to open for the music guests that will perform at Banjaara.

Rules

  • 3-10 members per group.
  • Cross-college participation is allowed as long as all team members are currently college students.
  • Only online pre-registration will be allowed.
  • There will be a maximum of 15 live performances.
  • Should more than 15 bands register for this event, all teams will go through an online preliminary round. Each band will have to submit a video in .wav or .mp4 format of 3-7 minutes of performance. All team members must be present in the video.
  • Each group will be given a total time of 15 minutes on-stage time, which includes set up.
  • A drum kit will be provided; all other instruments will have to be brought by teams themselves.
  • A minimum of one vocalist and one percussionist is compulsory.
  • Both covers and original compositions are allowed.
  • There will be Bonus points for creativity for original compositions and original arrangements.
  • If a group is performing an original composition, 3 copies of the lyrics must be submitted before hand.
  • Songs in languages other than English and Hindi are acceptable, however, a literal translation of the lyrics will have to be submitted in advance to the given email address.
  • Pre-recorded music / MIDI samples / sequencing / looping will lead to disqualification.
  • No backing tracks are allowed.
  • If a group overshoots the time limit, one point will be deducted for each extra minute.

Judging Criteria

  • Sur
  • Pitching
  • Rhythm
  • Choice of songs
  • Creativity
  • Quality of Music Arrangement
  • Overall Impact

Prizes and Fees

  • 1st Position - Prizes worth Rs.50,000
  • 2nd Position - Prizes worth Rs.15,000
  • Individual Registration Fee - Rs.350
