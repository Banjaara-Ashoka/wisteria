Brief

You, the stage, a single spotlight. The Vocal Solo is an event that allows to you showcase your raw singing talent, and belt out those jaw dropping notes to win prizes worth Rs. 12,000. There are two categories for the event, Indian and Western, and a maximum of 3 participants from one college can enter a single category. Participants are allowed to have only one accompanist, and get 6 minutes to showcase the best of their abilities.

Rules

  • Individual participation only.
  • Individuals can participate with one accompanist who performs live with them.
  • There are two categories within this event: Indian and Western. There is a limit of three entries per category per college, and six entries in total.
  • In the category of the Indian Solo, participants are allowed to perform semi-classical as well as Bollywood songs.
  • Only online pre-registration will be allowed.
  • Only a maximum of 8 participants will be allowed to perform live in each category.
  • Should more than 8 participants register for this event, there will be an online preliminary round. Each participant will have to submit a video in .wav or .mp4 format of 2-6 minutes of performance. The participant must be present in the video.
  • The deadline for the submission of the preliminary round is 28th January, 11:59 PM. Extended to 29th January.
  • Each participant will get 6 minutes of on-stage time, including set up.
  • Pre-recorded backing tracks are allowed.
  • If a participant overshoots the time limit, one point will be deducted for each extra minute.

Judging Criteria

  • Sur
  • Pitching
  • Rhythm
  • Choice of Songs
  • Overall Impact

Prizes and Fees

  • 1st Position (Indian) - Prizes worth Rs.12,000
  • 1st Position (Western) - Prizes worth Rs.12,000
  • 2nd Position (Indian) - Prizes worth Rs.8,000
  • 2nd Position (Western) - Prizes worth Rs.8,000
  • Individual Registration Fee - Rs.200
