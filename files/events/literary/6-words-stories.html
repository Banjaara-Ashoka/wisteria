Brief

Test the power of your words with this telegraphic story writing competition. Six-Word Stories, as the name ostensibly suggests, is a story-writing competition that challenges its participant to convey a rich tale in just six words. The top three stories will award their writers cash prizes of Rs. 4000 each!

Rules

  • Participants will perform individually in this event.
  • There is no limit on the number of participants from each college.
  • Both pre-registration and on-the-spot registration (on Day 1 from 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM) are possible.
  • A maximum of 20 participants will be allowed to participate in this event. Registrations will happen on a first-come-first-serve basis. Should all 20 participants register online, no on-spot registration will be available.
  • Every participant will have to write a story only six words long.
  • A Google form will be shared with the individuals who wish to participate, which they must use to send in their submissions.
  • A participant can send in a maximum of 3 entries.
  • Only original stories will be considered. We will be checking for plagiarism, and all inauthentic entries will be disqualified.
  • The deadline for submission is 4:00 PM on Day 1.

Judging Criteria

  • Creativity
  • Originality
  • Imagery
  • Syntax
  • Impact

Prizes and Fees

  • 1st Position - Prizes worth Rs.4,000
  • 2nd Position - Prizes worth Rs.4,000
  • 3rd Position - Prizes worth Rs.4,000
  • Individual Registration Fee - FREE!
