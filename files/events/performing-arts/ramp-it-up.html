Brief

Everybody always put a large emphasis on appearance, but at Banjaara we want you to look beyond the surface. This fashion show is an integration of self expression and creativity, asking of teams to come together and create unique designs related to a self-selected theme.Showcase your talents as both a creative director and a vogue model and wow us at Banjaara!

Rules

  • A maximum of twenty members per team.
  • A maximum of two teams per college.
  • This is a pre-registration event only.
  • A maximum of eight teams in total will take part in this event. The slots will be filled by a first-come-first-serve basis.
  • One team can have a maximum of fourteen models and six backstage help.
  • All teams must use a theme that is self-assigned.
  • Teams will be given a maximum of 10 minutes on stage to perform.

Judging Criteria

  • Choreography
  • Originality
  • Costumes
  • Music Coordination
  • Use of Theme
  • Creative and apt display of garments and accessories
  • Sequence and walk
  • Confidence

Prizes and Fees

  • 1st Position - Prizes worth Rs.50,000
  • 2nd Position - Prizes worth Rs.15,000
  • Individual Registration Fee - Rs.300
