Brief

We’re going old school hip-hop with this event. Welcome to The Throwdown - Banjaara's street dance-off, where we welcome you to show off your best pops, locks, and swagger for a shot at the grand prize worth Rs. 10,000. Unlike "professional" dance performances, this battle requires your raw talent, improvisational skills, and bucketloads of personality in order for you to woo the crowds and judges. Taking place in two rounds, you will be asked to dance for up to 90 seconds to show off your mad skills.
Ready, set, battle!

Rules

  • One or two members per team.
  • Cross-college participation is allowed as long as all team members are currently college students.
  • This is a pre-registration event only.
  • Teams will be given 60 to 90 seconds to perform.
  • There will two rounds that will progress in the ‘knock-outs’ fashion.
  • The first round will consist of four groups, with four teams each. The winners from each group will then face each other off in the second and final round.

Judging Criteria

  • Spontaneity
  • Co-ordination
  • Energy
  • Audience response

Prizes and Fees

  • 1st Position - Prizes worth Rs.10,000
  • 2nd Position - Prizes worth Rs.5,000
  • Individual Registration Fee - Rs.200
