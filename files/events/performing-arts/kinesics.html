Brief

From ballet to kuchipudi, hip-hop to bhangra, dance is storytelling. Movement becomes the language, while the body becomes the medium for story to emerge.
In this event, we ask teams to come tell us any story, any story, through the medium of dance. We give you the freedom to pick a style(s), costumes, props and music - but all we ask in return is that you move us, make us laugh or cry or scream or suffer. Do this effectively, and you could win a prize worth Rs. 35,000.

Rules

  • Each team can have 8-20 members.
  • A maximum of 2 teams per college.
  • This is a pre-registration event only.
  • The performance must portray a theme or have a storyline.
  • Teams will be given a total of 10 minutes onstage, including upto 1 minute for an introduction of the teamâ€™s chosen theme.
  • Only original choreography is permitted.
  • The styles used in the performance can be Indian and/or Western, or a fusion of multiple styles.
  • The use of props is permitted.
  • There will be a preliminary round for all participating teams.

Judging Criteria

  • Costume
  • Selection of Theme
  • Execution of Theme
  • Synchronisation
  • Choreography
  • Musicality

Submission Guidelines

  • Each team is requested to send any 2-3 minutes of their performance in a .mp4 or .avi video to banjaara.submissions@ashoka.edu.in.
  • The video must show all participating members of the team.
  • The routine performed in the video must be the routine the team is planning to use in the competition. Teams cannot send in videos of differently choreographed performances.
  • The last date for submission is 25th January. Extended to 29th January.
  • We request you not to make any payments for registration for this event until you have received confirmation from us about your participation in the final round.
  • Should a selected team fail to pay the registration amount by 6th February, they will lose their spot and the invitation will be extended to the next, most qualified team immediately.
  • Only a maximum of eight teams will perform live at Banjaara.
