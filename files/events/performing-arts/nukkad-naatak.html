Brief

Suno, suno, bhaiyon aur behno! Banjaara announces its Street Play competition, for one and all.
Street theatre is integral to Indian culture. Since time immemorial, weâ€™ve had motley crowds of people gathered around street performers at the street corner. Today, we invite teams to come put up nukkad naataks to a large and diverse audience, to make their voices heard and to shine as actors. We want teams to use this classic performative art form and most effectively combine entertainment with education and social awareness. The winners of this competition will receive a prize worth Rs. 40,000.

Rules

  • 12-25 members per team.
  • 1 team per college.
  • This is a pre-registration event only.
  • All teams will have to take part in an online preliminary round.
  • The play can be only in English and/or Hindi.
  • The use of any electronic gadgets or instruments during the performance is not permitted.
  • Teams will perform for 20-25 minutes.
  • While props are permitted, we would recommend that the team uses them with discretion.
  • Teams will be negatively marked for non-compliance with these rules.

Judging Criteria

  • Content
  • Originality
  • Creativity
  • Performance

Submission Guidelines

  • Each team is requested to send any 10-15 minutes of their performance in a .mp4 or .avi video to banjaara.submissions@ashoka.edu.in. Teams should also send in their scripts in a .pdf file.
  • The play performed in the video must be the play the team is planning to use in the competition. Teams cannot send in videos of different performances.
  • The last date for submission is 25th January. Extended to 29th January.
  • We request you not to make any payments for registration for this event until you have received confirmation from us about your participation in the final round.
  • Should a selected team fail to pay the registration amount by 6th February, they will lose their spot and the invitation will be extended to the next, most qualified team immediately.
  • Only a maximum of ten teams will perform live at Banjaara.

Prizes and Fees

  • 1st Position - Prizes worth Rs.40,000
  • 2nd Position - Prizes worth Rs.10,000
  • Individual Registration Fee - Rs.200
