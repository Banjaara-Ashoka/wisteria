Brief

Shutterbug is a shoutout to all photographers who pride themselves on their ability to capture stories behind moments. All you have to do is capture the following themes in your photographs: things you see while travelling, capturing motion, one thing that you love about Ashoka University, and emotions. Subsequently, you have to submit one photograph for each theme, with respective captions to elucidate your creative depiction.

The catch is this: you only have 24 hours from the word go. Going the extra mile will be worth it, though, because the winner of this competition will receive a prize worth Rs. 10,000. Prove your salt as a photographer - capture the essence of Banjaara and win big!

Rules

  • Individual participation only.

  • This is an online registration event only.
  • A maximum of 15 participants will be allowed to participate in this event. Registrations will happen on a first-come-first-serve basis.
  • Participants will have a total time of 24 hours to click their pictures and submit them.

  • Participants must bring their own cameras, laptops and any other equipment.
  • Participants can only submit photographs of instances captured on campus.
  • Post processing/editing of any of the photographs is not allowed.

Submission Guidelines

  • Participants have to submit at least one, and a maximum two photographs, for each theme.
  • Each photograph must have a caption, which must be limited to one sentence with a maximum of 15 words.
  • Final submissions will be made via a Google Drive folder which will be shared with respective college teams.
  • The last date for submission is 25th January. Extended to 29th January.

Judging Criteria

  • Creativity
  • Originality
  • Power of theme/message chosen by the photographer
  • Technical Perfection

    • Colour/Tonal Rendition
    • Framing
    • Choice of viewpoint
  • Background
  • Visual Impact
