Brief

Is pop culture your jam? Are you a bibliomaniac? Have you always been intrigued by art forms? If the answer to any or all of these questions is yes, then this quiz is for you. Lone wolves and pairs are welcome to participate in Banjaara’s MELA (Music, Entertainment, Literature, Arts) Quiz, and stand a chance to win a prize worth Rs.10,000. May the force be with you.

Rules

  • Teams of upto 3 members.
  • Cross-college participation is allowed as long as all team members are currently college students.
  • Online registration will take place prior to the Fest.
  • The quiz will be conducted in two rounds.
  • The first round will be a 30-minute long, written preliminary round.
  • There will be a 30-minute break after the first round.
  • The top six teams will qualify for the final round.
  • The final round will be take place in the ‘pounce and bounce’ format.
  • The duration of the final round will be 1 hour and 30 minutes.

Prizes and Fees

  • 1st Position - Prizes worth Rs.10,000
  • 2nd Position - Prizes worth Rs.5,000
  • Individual Registration Fee - FREE!
