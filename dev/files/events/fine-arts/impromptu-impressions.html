Brief

Put your artistic skills to the test with Quick Sketches. This event is a perfect blend of observation, improvisation and flourish. Participants will be asked to sketch the goings - on of Banjaara —it could be a competitive event, a detour, a party or any other scene of the artist’s choosing. The winner will receive a prize worth Rs. 5000 and have the opportunity to display their winning work on the second day of the fest.

Rules

  • This competition is open only to individual participants.
  • Pre-registration and on-the-spot registration will be available.
  • Each college is allowed 5 entries
  • The event will start at 12 noon on Day 1.
  • Three A3 sheets will be provided on the time of registration, but only one will be accepted as the final submission from each participant.
  • Artists will be asked to submit their pieces between 5:00 PM and 7:00 PM on Day 1.
  • Any medium of art can be used, but participants have to carry their own materials. Only the A3 sheets will be provided.

Judging Criteria

  • Creativity
  • Originality
  • Display of techniques
  • Accuracy
  • Choice of subject

Prizes and Fees

  • 1st Position - Prizes worth Rs.5,000
  • 2nd Position - Prizes worth Rs.3,000
  • Individual Registration Fee - FREE!
