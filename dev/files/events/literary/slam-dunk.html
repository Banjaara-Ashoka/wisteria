Brief

Slam poetry is the form of creative expression that has recently taken the world by storm. With no props, music or costumes, it lets you be you on stage, and express your thoughts in the form of spoken word, regardless of how vulnerable, hilarious or outrageous they are. Slam Dunk is an opportunity for all the poets (and non-poets) to come and woo our judges and audiences with the performance of original poetry - and you never know, you could go back Rs. 12,000 richer.

Rules

  • Participants can perform individually or in groups.
  • Maximum of 5 members per group.
  • Cross-college participation is allowed as long as all team members are currently college students.
  • This event has online registration only.
  • A maximum of 15 participants will be allowed to take part in the live round.
  • All participants will have to go through an online preliminary round.
  • The time limit for the performance is 2-4 minutes.
  • Only original pieces will be accepted.
  • Only English poems will be accepted for the competition.

Judging Criteria

  • Performance
  • Content
  • Impact
  • Articulation

Submission Guidelines

  • All participants will be required to submit an audio file in .wav or .mp3 format of the poem they are performing, along with a written PDF of the same.
  • All submissions are to be sent via email to banjaara.submissions@ashoka.edu.in
  • The submission deadline is 6th February, 2017. Please note that this is also the last day to pay registration fees for Banjaara.
  • We request all participants to pay the registration fee for Slam Dunk by 6th February. Those who are not selected to perform in the live round will be refunded the total registration amount of this event.
  • All participants will definitively hear back from the Submissions committee by 8th February, 2017, regarding their selection into the final round.

Prizes and Fees

  • 1st Position - Prizes worth Rs.12,000
  • 2nd Position - Prizes worth Rs.8,000
  • Individual Registration Fee - Rs.200
