Brief

If words paint pictures, here is your chance to make them a movie! Teams of two can participate in this literary film event to bring to life a pre-assigned poem using Banjaara as their backdrop. These poems capture the longing of freedom and the desire for fun that Banjaara embodies. We want you to capture those themes of the poem while also encapsulating the vibe of the fest itself. It is a filmic challenge of imagination, of working with little to produce something impactful. The winning film will be broadcasted during the festival, and will win itâ€™s creators a prize worth Rs. 10,000.

Rules

  • Each team can have up to 2 members.
  • Cross-college participation is allowed as long as all team members are currently college students.
  • Pre-registration, as well as on-spot registration on Day 1 from 11:00 AM to 13:00 PM, is available.
  • A maximum of 15 participants will be allowed to participate in this event. Registrations will happen on a first-come-first-serve basis. Should all 15 participants register online, no on-spot registration will be available.
  • The movie has to be filmed on campus, during Banjaara.
  • The film has to be a minimum of 2 minutes and a maximum of 8 minutes.
  • Teams are only allowed to use the poem that will be shared with them via email; no other form of dialogue or recitation will be permitted.
  • Participants are allowed to entask other individuals to act or participate in the film, but are entirely responsible for the film-making and editing process.
  • The film has to be submitted through web transfer (or email) by 4 PM on Day 2.
  • Teams will have to bring their own recording and processing equipment i.e. phones, cameras, laptops, other.

Judging Criteria

  • Originality
  • Content and Creativity
  • Selection and Application of Materials
  • Impact

Prizes and Fees

  • 1st Position - Prizes worth Rs.10,000
  • 2nd Position - Prizes worth Rs.5,000
  • Individual Registration Fee - Rs.200
