Brief

Here is your chance to play in the major leagues of the corporate world. Teams of three will be asked to tackle problems that big businesses run into. Corporates will present problem statements in their fields/companies to all the participants. Participants will pick their problems in a lucky draw and will get two hours to ideate on solutions - but not alone. Teams get the opportunity to work with some of the smartest entrepreneurs today. Each team will be assigned a mentor who is an established corporate, like the founders of naukri.com and makemytrip.com. You will get 5 minutes to pitch to the judges and seal your fate as the winner of this competition. And big as the challenge, the reward is sweeter: the winning team earns a prize worth Rs. 10,000 and a personalized mentoring session with an Ashoka University founder.

Rules

  • 3 members per team.
  • Cross-college participation is allowed as long as all team members are currently college students.
  • Registration happens beforehand online.
  • Each team will pick their problem from a lucky draw. The order of drawing will be disclosed on the day, but will be random ordering.
  • After being allotted a problem, each team will get two hours to ideate. In this time, they can make use of the expertise and advice of their assigned mentors.
  • At the end of the ideation time, teams will get 5 minutes to pitch their solutions to a panel of judges.

Judging Criteria

  • Creativity of the Solution
  • Ability to Implement and Scale the Solution

Prizes and Fees

  • 1st Position - Prizes worth Rs.10,000
  • 2nd Position - Prizes worth Rs.5,000
  • Individual Registration Fee - Rs.200
