Brief

All the world may be a stage, but here at Banjaara we want you to put up the play of a lifetime. Welcome to the Stage Play competition, where we invite teams to perform scripted plays for the maximum of one hour. Each team will be given the liberty to choose their own genre, theme, stage set-up, and nearly everything else. Their only memo will be to take the audience and judges on a narrative journey worth enough to win a prize of Rs. 40,000. The stakes are high, but there is nothing to lose - cue the drama!

Rules

  • Each team can have a maximum of 15 actors. There is no limit for the number of crew members.
  • A maximum 1 team per college.
  • This is a pre-registration event only.
  • All teams will have to take part in an online preliminary round.
  • Each team will be given a total of 60 minutes to perform their play. This time is inclusive of stage set-up, performance and stage clearance.
  • A warning bell shall be rung at the 50-minute mark, and an end bell at 60 minutes, after which points will be deducted. Upon exceeding 62 minutes, lights will be switched off.
  • A teamâ€™s time will begin as soon as they occupy the stage for set-up.
  • Only general lights will be provided. Each team can send one member to the lights panel once the team occupies the greenroom.
  • Teams are requested to carry their own laptop and soundtrack(s).
  • Teams are permitted to play live music.
  • The play can be in English or in Hindi.
  • Teams are requested to carry their own props. Ashoka University shall only be providing 2 chairs, 2 stools, 2 tables, 2 small wooden boxes.
  • Teams are requested to report to the green room at least 20 minutes before their time slot.
  • Teams running late shall not be provided any extra time, neither will they be given a green room. If you fail to report to the venue in time for your performance, your slot shall be given to the next team in waiting.
  • Teams will be negatively marked for non-compliance with these rules.

Judging Criteria

  • Content
  • Originality and creativity
  • Performance
  • Stage Set-Up and Use

Submission Guidelines

  • Each team is requested to send any 15-20 minutes of their performance in a .mp4 or .avi video to banjaara.submissions@ashoka.edu.in. Teams should also send in their scripts in a .pdf file.
  • The play performed in the video must be the play the team is planning to use in the competition. Teams cannot send in videos of different performances.
  • The last date for submission is 25th January. Extended to 29th January.
  • We request you not to make any payments for registration for this event until you have received confirmation from us about your participation in the final round.
  • Should a selected team fail to pay the registration amount by 6th February, they will lose their spot and the invitation will be extended to the next, most qualified team immediately.
  • Only a maximum of six teams will perform live at Banjaara.

Prizes and Fees

  • 1st Position - Prizes worth Rs.40,000
  • 2nd Position - Prizes worth Rs.10,000
  • Individual Registration Fee - Rs.200
