Footloose

Description

It is time to put on your dancing shoes and gather your team spirit! Whether your group’s style is western or Indian fold or even a fusion of the two, this is definitely the event for you.

24 February 2018, 2pm to 4pm at Main Stage

25 members per team

Maximum 2 teams per college

Registration Fee - Rs. 500 per team

For general queries, contact Meher Sachdeva at 9654194715 or Charvi Saraf at 8818800378, or email banjaara.events@ashoka.edu.in.

For queries on dance events, contact Aheli Gupta at 9748333062, or email banjaara.events@ashoka.edu.in with the subject line 'Dance Query'.


1st Prize : Rs. 25,000

2nd Prize : Rs. 15,000

Rules & Procedure

  1. Only 2 teams per college are allowed to participate.
  2. The team should have a maximum of 25 members and between 5-15 performers will be allowed on stage at any given point during the performance.
  3. The performance should be 6-8 minutes long.
  4. The soundtrack needs to be submitted in .mp3 format through email to banjaara.events@ashoka.edu.in. The subject line should be ‘Dance-Group’ and the body of the email must consist of participant name and institution name. The music must also be brought by the teams in a pendrive.
  5. Music can be of the choice of the respective teams. Remixes are also allowed.
  6. Costumes and/ or props are to be arranged by the participants, who have to inform the organizers about them in advance.
  7. 2 minutes of setup time will be given to each team.
  8. The props should not damage the dance floor in any manner.
  9. 1 member from each team is required to come for the sound check before the performance begins. No sound check will be allowed once the event begins.
  10. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.

Judging Criteria

  1. Team Co-ordination
  2. Creativity and originality of routine
  3. Costume and effective use of stage space
  4. Efficiency of prop usage (if used)
  5. Expressions / spirit / body language.

Ashoka Students, please note: You are welcome to participate but you are not allowed to compete. For those interested in participating, please contact the respective clubs and socities.

Stage Play

Description

They say that all the world’s a stage, but we believe that for theatre enthusiasts, the stage is but, their world. Keeping this in mind, Banjaara 2.0 invites all college teams to showcase a play and introduce the audiences to the extraordinary wonders of the stage. Don’t forget to pack in your set, your props and of course all the drama!

Date, Time, Venue - 25 February 2018, 11am to 4pm at Reddy’s Auditorium

10-15 members per team

Maximum 2 teams per college

Registration Fee - Rs. 600 per team

For general queries, contact Meher Sachdeva at 9654194715 or Charvi Saraf at 8818800378, or email banjaara.events@ashoka.edu.in.

For queries on theatre events, contact Trisha Sobti at 9911177067, or email banjaara.events@ashoka.edu.in with the subject line 'Theatre Query'.


Prelims

  1. A 10-15 minutes long prelim video clip of the performance will have to be sent to the email ID banjaara.events@ashoka.edu.in. The subject line must be: Prelims-Stage Play. The body of the email must consist of details such as team code (generated through registration), the cast size, college name, team members names, adaptation name (if an adapted play is used), due credits, along with a description of the play. The part which the team finds to be the best representation of the performance must be sent. The video clip must be fully inclusive of the required costumes and props that would be used for the main performance. The prelims have to be submitted by 13th February 2018.
  2. 5 top teams will be shortlisted, following which communication will be sent from the Formal Events Team.

1st Prize : Rs. 40,000

2nd Prize : Rs. 20,000

3rd Prize : Rs. 10,000

Rules & Procedure

  1. The costume, makeup and props required for the performances must be brought along by the respective teams. The host university will not be responsible for their arrangement or safety.
  2. The languages that can be used are English and Hindi.
  3. There will be negative marking for exceeding the time limit.
  4. There will be negative marking if the number of participants does not fit into the required bracket.
  5. There will be disqualification for explicit and/or offensive display of sexual harassment acts.
  6. Derogatory remarks targeting a particular social institution or entity such as a particular religion, caste, person, etc. will be deemed as defamation and will not be tolerated.
  7. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.

Final Performance Time - 40-50 minutes (inclusive of stage set-up time)


Judging Criteria

  1. Content and script
  2. Creative usage of theatrical elements (lights, props, sound, etc.)
  3. Fluency and voice modulation in dialogue delivery
  4. Clarity of thought and objective
  5. Physical expressions (body language, facial expressions, etc.)
  6. Costumes and set-design

Policies on Offensive Content

  1. There will be zero tolerance of explicit and/or offensive acts or language that abet any form of sexual harassment.
  2. Derogatory remarks targeting a particular social institution or entity such as a particular religion, caste, person, etc. will be deemed as defamation and will not be tolerated.

Ashoka Students, please note: You are welcome to participate but you are not allowed to compete. For those interested in participating, please contact the respective clubs and socities.

Battle of the Bands

Description

This event lends you 25 exclusive minutes to dazzle the stage with your beat, strum and hum. This battle gives the bands an opportunity to test their skills with competitors from eminent colleges from all across the country.

Date, Time, Venue - 25 February 2018, 10:30am to 3:30pm, Main Stage

3-10 members per team

Maximum 2 teams per college

Registration Fee - Rs. 800 per team

For general queries, contact Meher Sachdeva at 9654194715 or Charvi Saraf at 8818800378, or email banjaara.events@ashoka.edu.in.

For queries on the music events, contact Abhijit Sreepada at 9618670553, or email banjaara.events@ashoka.edu.in with the subject line 'Music Query'.


Prelims

  1. Prelims will be conducted.
  2. Video recordings in standard formats with all participating members or links to the same should be sent to banjaara.events@ashoka.edu.in with subject line as “Prelims BoB”. The email must mention the team code (generated through registration), name of the institute, name of the band (if any) and names of band members. The prelim must be submitted by 18th February 2018.
  3. Preliminary round results will be judged internally.

1st Prize : Rs. 40,000

2nd Prize : Rs. 20,000

3rd Prize : Rs. 10,000


Rules & Procedure

  1. Each band is expected to perform at least two songs. There is NO genre restriction. The songs may be original compositions or cover arrangements.
  2. A minimum of one vocalist and one percussionist is compulsory.
  3. No backing tracks are allowed.
  4. Original compositions as well as original arrangements get extra points for creativity.
  5. Pre-recorded music / MIDI samples / sequencing / looping will lead to disqualification.
  6. BONUS: Though not mandatory, the bands can earn some brownie points if they are able to give a “cross-genre” performance for one of the two songs. For this, they must choose an existing song, first perform the first part of the song in the original genre and then shift gears and change the genre of the song to another genre of their choice while ensuring the song does not lose its essence.
  7. Every extra minute taken post 25 minutes will result in a deduction of points.
  8. In case the band is performing an original composition, a document with the lyrics must be sent to banjaara.events@ashoka.edu.in after registration with the subject line ‘BoB - Original Lyrics’. The email must mention the name of the institute, name of the band (if any), names of band members, and name of the song.
  9. If a song is in a language other than English or Hindi, a document with the literal translation should be sent to banjaara.events@ashoka.edu.in with the subject line ‘BoB - Translation’. The email must mention the name of the institute, name of the band (if any), names of band members, song name and language of the song.

Performance Time - 25 minutes (including set-up time)


Judging Criteria

  1. Pitching
  2. Rhythm
  3. Choice of songs
  4. Creativity
  5. Quality of Music Arrangement
  6. Overall Impact
  7. BONUS: Cross Genre

Ashoka Students, please note: You are welcome to participate but you are not allowed to compete. For those interested in participating, please contact the respective clubs and socities.

Unplugged

Description

A unique event with utmost flexibility for different types of teams and bands and different types of performances. You can choose the genre of performance. Choirs, A Cappella groups, Unplugged Bands, all are welcome in this event.

Date, Time, Venue - 24 February 2018, 11am to 1:30pm, Main Amphitheatre

3-10 members per team

Registration Fee - Rs. 500 per team

For general queries, contact Meher Sachdeva at 9654194715 or Charvi Saraf at 8818800378, or email banjaara.events@ashoka.edu.in.

For queries on the music events, contact Abhijit Sreepada at 9618670553, or email banjaara.events@ashoka.edu.in with the subject line 'Music Query'.

2nd Prize : Rs. 15,000

  1. The team is free to perform more than one song, a mashup, a medley or anything that would compliment the theme they may choose to depict.

  3. Pre-recorded music / MIDI samples / sequencing / looping will lead to disqualification.

Pitchfork

Description

Banjaara 2.0 presents a panel of investors, the 'Sharks', who will listen to pitches from your entrepreneurial self seeking to gain some funding!

Date, Time, Venue - 25 February 2017, 11am to 4pm at the Centre for Entrepreneurship (CFE)

2-3 members per team

Registration Fee - Rs. 150 per team

For general queries, contact Meher Sachdeva at 9654194715 or Charvi Saraf at 8818800378, or email banjaara.events@ashoka.edu.in.

For queries on PitchFork, contact Akshat Praneet at 9776625300, or email banjaara.events@ashoka.edu.in with the subject line 'Entrepreneurship Query'.


Prelims

  1. There must be 2-3 members per team.
  2. The teams are required to send Business Model Canvas (BMC) and a one page description of the idea to banjaara.events@ashoka.edu.in by 15th February. The subject line of the email should be ‘Prelims - PitchFork’. The body of the email must mention Team name, participants names, institute’s name.
  3. The judging criteria of the prelims will be viability, innovation, and appropriate use of BMC.
  4. The shortlisted teams are to pay Rs. 150 to appear in finals.

  1. All shortlisted teams will be sent a template of the presentation and a cost analysis sheet.

  3. Post presentation,if there are any investment offers from the panel, there will be 5 minutes for negotiation.
  4. All teams have to turn in their presentations and cost analysis sheets an hour before the event starts, via email.

Performance time - 7 minutes presentation + 5 minutes negotiation


Judging Criteria

  1. Communication (presentation, clarity of idea)
  2. Innovation
  3. X-Factor
  4. Seed evaluation

Individual performances

Hello World!

Description

Whether you’re a skilled coder, or just starting out, this event is a chance for you to test your knowledge! This event is an ACM ICPC styled coding competition for teams of 1-3 members, where each team must write solutions in either C, C++ or Python for 5 questions of varying difficulty levels.

Date, Time, Venue - 24 February 2018, 11am to 3pm in the New academic block (010)

1-3 members per team

Registration Fee - Rs. 150 per team

For general queries, contact Meher Sachdeva at 9654194715 or Charvi Saraf at 8818800378, or email banjaara.events@ashoka.edu.in.

For queries on the computing event, ‘Hello World!’, contact Vasudha Malani at 9923922187, or email banjaara.events@ashoka.edu.in with the subject line 'Computing Query'.

1st Prize : Rs. 12,000

2nd Prize : Rs. 5,000


Rules & Procedure

  1. Each participant is expected to get their own laptops to work on.
  2. The event is an ACM ICPC styled coding event for teams of up to 3 members.
  3. You will receive 5 questions of varying difficulty levels that you have to code up solutions to.
  4. You will receive one point for passing all test cases, regardless of the level of difficulty of that problem.
  5. The teams will be ranked according to the most problems solved. Ties will be broken by the total time for each user in ascending order of time.
  6. The total time is the sum of the time consumed for each problem solved. The time consumed for a solved problem is the time elapsed from the beginning of the contest to the submittal of the first accepted run plus 20 penalty minutes for every previously rejected run for that problem. There is no time consumed for a problem that is not solved.

Total time - 3 hours

Ashoka Students, please note: You are welcome to participate but you are not allowed to compete. For those interested in participating, please contact the respective clubs and socities.
