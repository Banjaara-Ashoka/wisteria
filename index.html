About Banjaara

Banjaara is Ashoka's annual cultural festival, one of our flagship events, which in the past has experiences a footfall of 2000+ people. Now entering its third year, the event in 2019 promises to be our best one yet. Packed with inter-collegiate formal events ranging across music, dance, theatre, and more, and diverse informal events that keep attendees engages and entertained throughout. Banjaara has something for everyone! This year we bring you entertainment that is twice as good! Come one and come all, to be a part of this carnival of magic!
