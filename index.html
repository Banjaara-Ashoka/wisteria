Events

Banjaara is about adventure and new experiences. So drop by our People Stops and make a memory! Think of them as milestones in your Banjaara journey: all the activities you complete will lead you to the ultimate prize - a sponsored trek to the Himalayas! Learn how to make something new in less than 5 minutes, battle it out for ultimate Lip Sync dominance, and collect quirky memorabilia along the way. Who knows, you might even make some new friends in the bargain.

P.S: Keep an eye out for our Pop Out Stalls; they might show up when least expected.

Parties

Neon Party
We can't stop, we won't stop - make that your anthem for this fest, because Banjaara presents two exceptional parties that will make staying up all night feel like a breeze.

Get (g)low for the neon party - the first in the line-up of our nocturnal activities - where you can let loose with some chill music and go wild with glow-in-the-dark paint. This space promises surprises for the wallflowers too, who don't like to bust a move: expect neon ping-pong and other fun activities! Make the most of this lit space, because the neon party will close as the sun rises.

But worry not! Step out of the 90s Rave scene and into the evergreen filmi one, because our next party is...drumroll, please...Band Baaja Bollywood. Regardless of how "global" we become, nothing gets us moving and grooving like Bollywood beats. So break out those thumkas and shimmies and dance it up with friends, enemies and strangers, because jab tak humare pair chalenge, tab tak humari saans chalegi (Sholay, anyone?).

Zones

Art Zone:
Envisioned as a creative space, feel free to use the art zone to go crazy and unleash your inner genius. We’ve set this up for you for the duration of the fest, so do leave us with your masterpieces! For those still hesitant about their art we have tye dye painting, paint angels and origami among other activities to help ease you into the crazy world of Banjaara.

Street Zone:
In the style of ‘Equal Streets’, we present to you the Banjaara Street Zone. Here’s where you’ll get your fix of food and drink - let our line of food trucks cater to your foodie dreams. Watch out for our funky informals! Hint:Cycling isn’t always a race. As a bonus, you can doodle on the road with brightly coloured chalk while you wait for your order. You can thank us later.

Carnival Haat:
“You can’t have everything”. Wrong. The Banjaara Carnival-Haat is the perfect blend of East and West. It’s a diner-meets-dhaba, Starbucks-meets-Ramu Kaka’s chai stall, and a desi-meets-videshi khichdi of all your favourite things. We prove that we can explore the wild West while still staying connected to our roots. Head on over to our Haat - the artisans are waiting to help you rediscover the joy in small, desi things like mehendi and pagdis. On the other hand, there are stuffed toys to be won and games to be played at our Carnival. Don’t forget to grab some candyfloss to go with your jalebis. Enjoy our modern jugalbandi!

Reach Ashoka

Apart from your private vehicles, Delhi Metro is your best choice. Get down on Jahangirpuri Metro Station (yellow line). From there, you will be able to board the University Shuttle to reach Ashoka (this is a 40 minutes ride). Boarding point is the area between Gate No. 3 & 4 at Jahangirpuri Metro Station, opposite KDR wools (glass building).
The same shuttles will drop you back after Banjaara.
