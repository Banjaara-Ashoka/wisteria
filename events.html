Coliseum - Stage Play
  • Details
  • Prizes & Judging Criteria

This event is all about the drama! Showcase your acting skills through a well-crafted and impactfully delivered play. Take the stage with your team and introduce the audience to the extraordinary wonders of theatre!

Date: 23 February
Time: 10:00am - 04:00pm
Venue: Reddy's Auditorium


Rules

Team Size: On stage: min. 2, max. no cap; Off-stage crew assistants: 4
Teams per College: 2
Registration Fee: INR 800

Preliminary Round (Online)
  • • The prelims will be judged internally through 12-15 minute video clips.
  • • The videos must be sent to the email ID banjaara.events@ashoka.edu.in. The subject line must be ‘Prelims- Stage Play’. The body of the email must consist of the following information:
    •    - Name of Institution
    •    - Name of the Play
    •    - Team name (if any)
    •    - Synopsis of the play
    •    - A list of props that will be used by the team
    •    - Cast list
    •    - Name of the adaption (if an adapted play is used)
  • • The last date for submission of prelim video clips will be 14th February, 2019. Results will be announced by 20th February.

General Rules
  • • Each team will get a maximum of 50 minutes (including set-up) to perform.
  • • The host University will provide:
    •    - An audio set
    •    - Lights Control Panel
    •    - Optional props- 3 chairs and 2 tables
  • • Any other props must be brought along by the participants. Ashoka University reserves the right to deny prop usage if it can cause permanent damage to property.
  • • The costume, makeup and props required for the performances must be brought along by the respective teams. The host university will not be responsible for their safety.
  • • Teams will be provided with a green room for changing and storage.
  • • The languages that can be used are English, Hindi or a mix of both.
  • • There will be negative marking for exceeding the time limit.
  • • The judges’ decision will be final and abiding.

Prizes

  • • 1st Position: INR 50,000
  • • 1st Position: INR 20,000
  • • 1st Position: INR 10,000

Judging Criteria
  • • Acting
  • • Content
  • • Direction
  • • Music
  • • Lights

Nutcracker - Street Dance (Western)

  • Details
  • Prizes & Judging Criteria

Dance your heart out in styles such as hip hop, breaking, freestyle, locking, popping. Come show us your passion for dance as you jump, flip, and groove to victory in this group event!

Date: 24 February
Time: 10:30am - 01:30pm (3 hours)
Venue: Basketball Court


Rules

Team Size: 5-18
Teams per College: 2
Registration Fee: INR 500
Styles: The choreography performed must be within the western dance sphere, with forms such as hip hop, popping, locking, freestyle, tutting, krumping, breaking, wacking.

Preliminary Round (Online)
  • • All teams registering will be required to submit a 3-5 minute video of their dance.
  • • The video must be submitted in .mp4 format.
  • • The video must be mailed to banjaara.events@ashoka.edu.in by 14th February, 2019. The subject line of this email should be ‘Prelims- Street Dance’. The results will be announced by 20th February.
  • • The body of the email must consist of the team name, name and number of members, and institution name. The video should consist of whichever part of the dance the performers find most suitable for auditions. Performing in full costume and with props is preferred.

General Rules
  • • Only 2 teams per college are allowed to participate.
  • • The strength of a team should be between 5 to 18 members.
  • • Selected teams will be required to perform their full choreography of 6-8 minutes, with 2 extra minutes for setup.
  • • All teams selected must submit their audio tracks (mp3 format) in advance via mail and must carry them on a pendrive on the day of the competition as well.
  • • All props used need to be informed about in advance. Participants must bring their own props, the university will not provide any. Hazardous props like flammables and sharp objects will not be allowed.
  • • The time limit as clearly stated above must be kept in mind, exceeding them will lead to deduction of points.
  • • There is no bar on the language of songs used. Participants are free to use as many songs as they want, they can also mix songs.
  • • The judges’ decision will be final and abiding.

Prizes: Prize money worth INR 45,000


Judging Criteria
  • • Choreography
  • • Costume
  • • Creativity
  • • Musicality
  • • Energy
  • • Stage/Space utilization
  • • Overall impact
Nutcracker - Folk Dance
  • Details
  • Prizes & Judging Criteria

Taking from India's rich tradition, immense diversity and years of heritage, we urge you to come forward and present the myriad of colors and beauty that the energetic folk dances reflect. Give your audience a flavour of the cultures that surround them in this group dance competition!

Date: 23 February
Time: 11:00am - 12:30pm (1.5 hours)
Venue: Main Stage


Rules

Team Size: 6-15
Teams per College: 2
Registration Fee: INR 500

Preliminary Round (Online)
  • • All teams registering will be required to submit a 3-4 minute video of their dance.
  • • The video must be in .mp4 format.
  • • The teams can choose to perform any folk or traditional dance. However, the piece has to be strictly non-classical. The teams will have to continue with the same form if they are selected.
  • • The video must be mailed to banjaara.events@ashoka.edu.in by 14th February, 2019. The subject line of this email should be ‘Prelims- Folk Dance’. The results will be announced by 20th February.
  • • The body of the email must consist of the team name, name and number of members, and the institution name. The video must consist of whichever part of the dance the performers find most suitable for auditions.
  • • Performing in full costume and with props is preferred.

General Rules
  • • Only 2 teams per college are allowed to participate.
  • • The strength of a team should be 6 to 15 members.
  • • On the day of the competition selected teams will be required to present a 6-8 minutes long performance on stage in front of an audience and judges.
  • • All performances should be within the arena of folk dance (Ghumar, Bhangra, Garba, Chhau, etc.), and all choreography should be strictly non-classical.
  • • All teams selected must submit an audio track of their performance in .mp3 format to the email id banjaara.events@ashoka.edu.in. They must carry the sound track on a pen drive on the day of the event as well.
  • • Exceeding the time limit will lead to a deduction of points.
  • • All teams using props are required to inform so in advance. Participants must bring their own props, the university will not provide any. Hazardous props like flammables and sharp objects will not be allowed.
  • • The judges’ decision will be final and abiding.

Prizes: Prize money worth INR 45,000


Judging Criteria
  • • Choreography
  • • Technique (transitions, coordination, authentic to the folk style, expressions)
  • • Prop usage
  • • Costumes
  • • Stage utilization
  • • Energy
  • • Overall impact
Battle of Bands
  • Details
  • Prizes & Judging Criteria

This competition will have different bands battle it out to see who can play the best music. Singers, guitarist, pianists, percussionists, this event is for all of you!

Date: 24 February
Time: 01:30pm - 04:30pm
Venue: Main Stage


Rules

Team Size: 3-8
Teams per College: 2 Finalists from each college (as many can apply for prelims)
Registration Fee: INR 800

Preliminary Round (Online)
  • • The prelims will be judged internally through 4-5 minute video clips.
  • • The video must include all members of the group.
  • • Audio may be recorded into a software and the volume levels adjusted with the faders. No effects will be allowed post recording (EQ, delay etc.).
  • • The video and audio quality should be of a decent level in order to facilitate a smoother judging procedure. Entries with below-average quality stand the chance of not being considered.
  • • The videos must be sent to the email ID banjaara.events@ashoka.edu.in. The subject line must be ‘Prelims- BoB’. The body of the email must consist of the band name, institution name, and the number and names of all team members.
  • • The last date for submission of prelim video clips will be 14th February, 2019. Results will be announced by 20th February.

General Rules
  • • There is no restriction on the languages used.
  • • A vocalist is compulsory.
  • • No backing tracks are allowed.
  • • Any pre-recorded material/MIDI samples/sequencing will lead to direct disqualification.
  • • Each team will get a maximum of 25 minutes to perform. This includes set-up time. Extra time taken will result in a deduction of points in 30-second intervals, corresponding to the lower boundary exceeded (ie. if a team goes one second over 25 minutes, points are deducted. Additionally, if the team goes a second above 1 minute, another set of points will be deducted).
  • • In case the band is performing an original composition, a document with the lyrics must be sent to banjaara.events@ashoka.edu.in after registration with the subject line ‘BoB - Original Lyrics’. The email must mention the name of the institute, name of the band (if any), names of band members, and name of the song.
  • • If a song is in a language other than English or Hindi, a document with the literal translation should be sent to banjaara.events@ashoka.edu.in with the subject line ‘BoB - Translation’. The email must mention the name of the institute, name of the band (if any), names of band members, song name and language of the song.
  • • The judges’ decision will be final and abiding.

Prizes: Prize money worth INR 80,000


Judging Criteria
  • • Pitch
  • • Harmony
  • • Syncronisation
  • • Technicality
  • • Stage Performance
  • • Creative interpretation- covers of songs reimagined creatively, improvisation, etc. will be appreciated
Western Choir
  • Details
  • Prizes & Judging Criteria

If you can sing and harmonize with a group, this is your time to shine! Compete with teams to see which can best perform English songs in a choir.

Date: 24 February
Time: 10:30am - 01:00pm
Venue: Reddy's Auditorium


Rules

Team Size: 6-20
Teams per College: 2
Registration Fee: INR 800

Preliminary Round (Online)
  • • The prelims will be judged internally through 4-5 minute video clips.
  • • The recording must include all the members of the group.
  • • The video recording should not be in any way edited. Recording into a software and putting that audio over the video is allowed but the audio recorded is not to be tampered with.
  • • The videos must be sent to the email ID banjaara.events@ashoka.edu.in. The subject line must be ‘Prelims- Western Choir’. The body of the email must consist of the institution name, team name (if any), and the number and names of all team members.
  • • The last date for submission of prelim video clips will be 14th February, 2019. Results will be announced by the 20th February.

General Rules
  • • Removing mics from mic stands is not allowed.
  • • No backing tracks are allowed.
  • • Only English songs are permitted in this competition.
  • • Pre-recorded tracks, midi samples and sequencing are not allowed; use of the same may lead to disqualification. Looping of a single instrument, like a guitar or a keyboard, is allowed. Additionally, the ‘comp’, ‘style’ and ‘song’ features in a keyboard are also not allowed.
  • • Originals are permitted but they will be judged on the same parameters. If the group chooses to perform an original composition they must mail the lyrics to banjaara.events@ashoka.edu.in after registration. The mail should also include the name of the composition, the name of the group and the name of the institute they are representing.
  • • Performances must range between 10-12 mins. The performance must not exceed the given time limit. Extra time taken will result in a deduction of points in 30-second intervals, corresponding to the higher boundary exceeded.
  • • The judges’ decision will be final and abiding.

Prizes: Prize money worth INR 50,000


Judging Criteria
  • • Pitch
  • • Rhythm
  • • Harmony
  • • Coordination/Syncronisation
  • • Technicality
  • • Performance
